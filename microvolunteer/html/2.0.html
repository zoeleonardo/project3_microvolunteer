Nob Hill Senior Connections

Description

Make a seniors day while making meaningful connections in your neighborhood. We have several older adults in the Nob Hill neighborhood looking for a friendly visitor! (During COVID-19 affected times, we will begin our visits over the phone, then transition to in-person visits when the pandemic has passed).

Skills Required

free background check; availability for our next new volunteer training, bilingual volunteers strongly encouraged
